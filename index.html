About the Initiative

My Blood Is Yours is a non-profit humanitarian initiative designed to connect blood donors with patients in urgent need across Lebanon.

The project was founded and developed by Engineer Mohamad Mahmoud, believing that technology can become a bridge between donors and patients when every second matters.

دمنا واحد مبادرة إنسانية غير ربحية تهدف إلى ربط متبرعي الدم بالمرضى المحتاجين بسرعة وسهولة في مختلف المناطق اللبنانية.

In Loving Memory | إهداء
